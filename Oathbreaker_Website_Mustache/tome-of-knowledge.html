The Shadowmere is a treacherous region where conventional navigation fails. Seek the glowing wisps that mark safe passages, and avoid the crimson mists at all costs. If truly lost, use a Wayfinder's Stone to return to the last sanctuary you visited.

Many gates require specific keys, runes, or completed quests to open. Examine nearby inscriptions for clues, speak with local inhabitants, and explore thoroughly. Some doors only reveal themselves during certain moon phases or after defeating nearby guardians.

Sanctuaries are marked by golden braziers and the symbol of the Oathkeepers. Major settlements, hidden shrines, and ancient waypoints all provide respite. Resting at these locations will restore your vitality and allow you to commune with distant allies.

üìú Account & Chronicle Matters

If you've lost access to your chronicle, seek the Archivists through our support channels. Provide the email bound to your account and any identifying information. The Archivists will verify your identity and restore your access within 48 hours.

Cross-realm transfers are possible through the Chronicle Binding ritual. Navigate to your account settings, select 'Bind Chronicles,' and follow the sacred protocols. Note that some realm-specific achievements may not transfer.

Visit the Mirror of Reflection in any major settlement. For a modest offering of gold, you may alter your appearance. Name changes require a Scroll of Identity, obtainable through the in-game shop or special events.

ü§ù Guilds & Fellowship

To form a guild, gather five like-minded warriors and visit the Guild Registrar in any capital city. Pay the founding fee and choose your banner. To join an existing guild, seek out guild recruiters or use the Guild Finder in the social menu.

Guild members gain access to shared resources, exclusive quests, guild halls, and coordinated raid opportunities. Higher-ranked members can unlock special titles, cosmetic rewards, and the ability to participate in inter-guild tournaments.

Use the Whisperstone system to send private messages to allies regardless of location. Join voice channels through the party menu for real-time coordination. Guild members also have access to dedicated guild channels and announcement systems.
