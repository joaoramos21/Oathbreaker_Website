Our Mission

Forging Legends, One Story at a Time

At Oathbreaker Studios, we believe that games should be more than entertainment—they should be transformative experiences that ignite imagination and forge unforgettable memories. Our mission is to craft immersive worlds where every player becomes the hero of their own epic tale.

We are dedicated to pushing the boundaries of interactive storytelling, blending rich lore, challenging gameplay, and stunning visuals to create experiences that resonate long after the final battle is won.

Our Story

Born from Passion and Shadow

Oathbreaker began as a shared dream among a small group of passionate developers who believed that the best games come from authentic creative vision. Founded in 2020, our studio has grown from a handful of dreamers into a dedicated team united by our love for fantasy, mythology, and epic adventures.

Every member of our team brings unique expertise and unwavering dedication to their craft. Together, we've built not just a game, but a living world that continues to evolve with our community.

Our Values

The Pillars of Our Realm

Creativity

We embrace bold ideas and encourage creative risk-taking. The most memorable moments come from daring to imagine the impossible and pushing the boundaries of interactive storytelling.

Community

Our players are at the heart of everything we do. We listen, engage, and build together with the community that makes Oathbreaker possible, fostering a collaborative environment where every voice matters.

Transparency

We believe in open communication and honest dialogue. From development updates to decision-making processes, we keep our community informed and involved in shaping the future of Oathbreaker.
