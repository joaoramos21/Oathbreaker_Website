Before steel, before magic — there were Oaths.

In the beginning, the world was bound by sacred vows. Mortals swore Oaths to ideals greater than themselves — virtues that shaped kingdoms and sins that shattered them.

When the first Oath was broken, the world fractured. From that fracture emerged the Vessels — living manifestations of belief twisted by failure, pride, hunger, and regret.
